ChessPairing is released under a custom non-commercial license.

## Summary

- **Personal use** -- free and unrestricted. Study the code, build from source, experiment.
- **Chess clubs** -- free for all club activities, including income-generating events (entry fees, sponsorships, ticket sales). Your club must be affiliated with a national chess federation that is a FIDE member.
- **Commercial use** -- not permitted without prior agreement. Companies and commercial platforms must contact the author.
- **Distribution** -- unmodified copies (source or binary) may be shared with the license and a link to the original source. Modified versions may not be distributed.
- **Contributions** -- intellectual property rights for accepted contributions transfer to the author. Contributors are credited by name in CONTRIBUTORS.md.
