**"Personal Use"** means using the Software as an individual for non-commercial

**"Recognized Chess Club"** is a chess club that is registered with, affiliated
with, or recognized by a national chess federation that is a member of FIDE (the
International Chess Federation). Not sure if your club qualifies? It probably
does. If your club is part of your country's official chess federation, you are
fine.

**"Club Activities"** are the things chess clubs do: organizing tournaments,
leagues, and matches; collecting entry fees or admission charges; arranging
sponsorships for events; publishing results and ratings; and everything else that
